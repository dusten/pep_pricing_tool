# TB-500 Fragment (17-23) Peptide Overview — PickPeptides

**Origin:** `raw/clippings/TB-500 Fragment (17-23) Peptide Overview.md`
**Source URL:** https://pickpeptides.com/peptides/tb-500-fragment-17-23/

## Key takeaways

- Confirms CAS 885340-08-9 for the acetylated fragment (Ac-LKKTETQ); also known as fequesetide
- PubChem CID 10169788; MW 846.97 g/mol (C₃₆H₆₆N₁₀O₁₃)
- MW distinction: Fragment (846.97 g/mol) vs TB-500 with aldehyde modification (889 Da); aldehyde form less stable
- Available as acetate (most common, ≥95% HPLC) or TFA salt
- Strong preclinical support: wound healing, angiogenesis, muscle/tendon repair across multiple PMID studies
- NOT FDA approved; research compound only
