# TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4) Peptide Overview — PickPeptides

**Source URL:** https://pickpeptides.com/peptides/tb-500-thymosin-beta-4/

## Key takeaways

- Overview describes TB-500 as the 7aa fragment (Ac-LKKTETQ), not the full 43aa TB4 — consistent with standard vendor usage
- UNII QHK6Z47GTG; acetate salt most common; also available as free base or other salts
- Extensive preclinical data: 42–61% wound healing acceleration, 2–3× keratinocyte migration enhancement
- NOT FDA approved; research compound only as of Oct 2025
