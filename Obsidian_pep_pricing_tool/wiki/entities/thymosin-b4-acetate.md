## Notes

- Naturally occurring in nearly all human and animal tissues; abundant intracellular actin-sequestering protein
- Broader mechanism than the fragment: actin sequestration, cell migration, angiogenesis, stem cell recruitment, immune modulation, multi-organ regeneration
- Shorter half-life (~2h in some studies) than the 7aa fragment
- More expensive to synthesize; less stable for long-term storage
- Vendors sometimes mislabel the 7aa fragment as "TB-500" when listing this full compound — verify by MW
