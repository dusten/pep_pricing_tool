# TB-500 / Thymosin Beta Family

**⚠ High nomenclature risk** — "TB-500" in vendor listings almost always means the 7aa fragment (B5), not the full 43aa TB4. TB5 (CAS 948841-07-4) is a completely unrelated small molecule. CAS number is the only reliable differentiator.
