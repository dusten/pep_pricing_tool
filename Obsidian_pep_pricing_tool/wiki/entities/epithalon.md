# Epithalon (Epitalon)

Synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) originally developed by Professor Vladimir Khavinson at the Saint Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation and Gerontology. Primary mechanism: upregulation of telomerase (hTERT), telomere elongation, antioxidant and geroprotective effects.
