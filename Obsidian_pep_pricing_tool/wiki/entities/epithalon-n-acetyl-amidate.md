## Notes

- Dual protection against exopeptidases → significantly higher metabolic stability
- Extended plasma half-life; better systemic exposure
- ~10× lower effective dose than base (100–1,000 mcg/day vs 5–10 mg/day)
- Limited direct research studies; benefits inferred from base compound mechanism
- Solubility can be batch-dependent; gentle reconstitution recommended
- No official CAS — vendor CAS inconsistency is a red flag worth flagging
